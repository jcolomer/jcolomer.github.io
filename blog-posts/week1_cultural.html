Week 1: Initial impressions of DBC


Around 7 years ago, I came to the Bay Area interested in two main things: the entrepreneurial spirit of Silicon Valley and the craziness of San Francisco. I'm glad to say that my impression of DBC so far includes both attributes.

The importance of the first impression! ;)

I'm excited and happy to enjoy this opportunity, and -as always- I will do my best to honor the challenge.
