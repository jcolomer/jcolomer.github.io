How to ask good questions?


I find that the best question is the one you ask after having tried to answer it by yourself.

When you seriously try to find the answer to a problem or the right algorithm for a program, to postpone asking a question until you have exhausted all your attempts and sources to solve it makes you learn more and produce specific inquiries.

On the opposite side, "asking first" will help you finish your task earlier, but will probably decrease your learning ability.
