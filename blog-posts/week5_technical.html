Week 5 Technical

Classes

If you have to learn just one thing about Ruby, what you probably should know is that everything you manipulate in Ruby is an object. This highlights the importance of classes, since every object in Ruby is generated from a class.

In object-oriented design, you are normally looking to model concepts from the real world. In other words: you identify external things and make them classes in your code.
