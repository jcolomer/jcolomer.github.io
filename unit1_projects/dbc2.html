Years ago I was very surprised when I found out that Japanese girls cover their mouth when they laugh. Not doing it is considered unfemenine, disrespectful and rude in the traditional Japanese culture.

Other than gestures, it's interesting to see what make us laugh around the world. It seems like some jokes are international and some others are just for locals.
